About Dordoi Association

The Dordoi Association is one of the largest business organizations in Kyrgyzstan, established on December 5, 1991. It started with the opening of the Dordoi Market, a key wholesale trade center in Central Asia, located on the northern outskirts of Bishkek.

Development Timeline

1991: Establishment of the Dordoi Market, which became a major hub for wholesale trade in the region. 2001: Expansion into a multifaceted organization with a wide range of trade and production enterprises.
