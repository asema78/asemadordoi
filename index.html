About Us

The Dordoy Association is a leading public and business organization in Kyrgyzstan, bringing together individuals dedicated to the development and prosperity of the country. We actively contribute to economic growth, education, sports, culture, and social welfare. Our mission is to promote the sustainable development of Kyrgyzstan by creating opportunities for business growth, improving the quality of education, and fostering social responsibility.
