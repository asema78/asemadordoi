Dordoi

The "Dordoi" Association is actively involved in various significant projects and social initiatives, continuing to influence different spheres of life in Kyrgyzstan. Here's a more detailed description of some of their latest news and events:

Economic and Bisness initiatives

Infrastructure Development: The Association is actively developing business and infrastructure in Kyrgyzstan. In 2024, the construction of a large meat processing plant in the city of Balykchy began. This will be an important project for improving food security and providing local residents with jobs. The plant will meet high production standards and contribute to the growth of agriculture in the region.(Instagram) Support for Entrepreneurs: The Association works closely with small and medium-sized businesses, offering consultations, training, and assistance in establishing connections with other major market players. One of the directions is the promotion of Kyrgyz products to international markets through export.

Charity and Social Projects

Mobile Homes for the Batken Region: In October 2022, the Dordoy Association donated 10 mobile homes to residents of the Batken region, who were affected by natural disasters. These homes help restore housing in areas where traditional construction work is complicated due to geographical and climatic conditions. (Dordoi.kg) Koshoy Korgon Folk Art Festival: The Association allocated significant funds to hold this cultural event, which aims to preserve and promote the traditions of the Kyrgyz people. The festival attracted not only local residents but also foreign guests, facilitating the exchange of cultural achievements and strengthening connections between regions and cultures. (Tazabek.kg)

Opening of Educational institution

International IT and Business College: One of the most notable initiatives is the opening of the International IT and Business College in Bishkek. This college, launched in partnership with global institutions, provides a platform for students to learn in-demand skills in technology and business management. The institution focuses on offering world-class education that aligns with international standards, allowing students to be competitive both locally and globally. The college also offers programs aimed at bridging the gap between theory and practice, preparing graduates for the modern workforce. This project is particularly important in a rapidly digitalizing world, where IT and business skills are in high demand. (Dordoi.kg)
